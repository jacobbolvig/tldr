NiP slip up as f0rest is not oak-ay ahem

Source: StarLadder / Igor Bezborodov

Nothing in Paypal Ninjas in Pyjamas crashed to a 0-2 start, after a 10-16 loss to CR4ZY on Train. Despite Golden standing in, it was once again the experienced head and bushy beard of f0rest, who could only cut down 9 onrushing enemies. Life's a birch, and then you die. 21 times.

One can only assume the Swedish veteran has been busy looking after his new baby, and having brought new life into the world must feel pretty guilty about taking virtual lives out. At least there's one f0rest that isn't on fire.

The legendary Swedish organisation, that apparently went 87-0 at some point (though no living human has any recollection), face elimination as they take on North in an 0-2 game.

That G2 fail
 

What else happened?

They say everything in Australia is out to kill you - we can only assume that the period in NA has turned Renegades American. They were not ready for buster, the Kazakh killer, as he dropped 26 frags on everyone's favourite map, Vertigo, to sink Renegades into the 0-2 bracket.

Boombl4 chewed up and spat out the DreamEaters in a brutal Terrorist side performance. The Russian underdogs turned into a chew-toy for the new boy, as he rolled through their meagre CT holds. s1mple is alright too, we guess.

North battled back from a huge deficit just to run through a smoke into the grateful arms of chrisJ. The Terrifying Dutchman sprayed down four in a PaszaBiceps-esque highlight, to apply the Heimlich manoeuvre to a karrigan-led team (almost) choking on Inferno. Sound familiar?

According to Google, sloths sleep somewhere between 15-18 hours a day; NRG apparently caught NAF during this period. He posted only 7 frags in 25 rounds as CeRq drove a bulldozer through the world's number one team.

After a monster performance yesterday, rain couldn't weather the storm that Vitality brought to the sun-soaked Dust II. The Norwegian had a seriously slow start and Vitality punished him. Instead, the forecast was 'cloudy with a chance of ZywOo killing everyone in sight'.

It was... sigh... easy for ENCE against MIBR. Despite TACO turning into coldzera 2.0 (crazy what you can do, when you are the one doing the baiting), Aleksib sent a message to his teammates with a stellar performance. Still no smile, though..

Here at tl;dr, we believe the CT side is much easier when you defuse the bomb. G2, on the other hand, don't. With four of them alive in the final round they just refused to defuse. Not like the French to surrender. Apparently they don't sell defuse kits at g2esports.com/shop
