Team Envy drama

 

A Fugly situation

They say every friend group has a smaller group chat without one or two people who everyone secretly doesn't like. If you think your friend group doesn't have one, you might be the friend nobody likes. Nifty, on Envy, was that guy.

His teammates, according to Fugly's twitlonger, wanted rid of him, and decided to create a Whatsapp group chat to discuss their plans. We reckon it was called 'The Coup d'Envy'.

An artist's interpretation of what that fated chat might look like

Upon Nifty finding out about their Fawkes-ian plan to take out the leader from below, he told AWPer Sonic that he's gotta go fast.

Nifty cut Sonic without the other players' consent, and Fugly was told he had to move to Dallas or he was cut, even though it would cost him more in rent. He agreed, but after months of being ghosted, he found out he was being cut via a HLTV article.

Now, that's a horrible way to find out, but you did try to cut your teammate via a Whatsapp coup d'état, so it's a little karmic. 

That said, Nifty certainly doesn't come off in a great way. He was close enough to the owners that his teammates weren't comfortable with talking to the org about it, cut a teammate without any consultation and then lied to and ghosted his teammate like a clingy one-night stand.

Not that we at TLDR would know anything about that, of course.

Don't worry, though, Envy. At least your old teammates will stick up for you as such a great orga-

GuardiaN to leave NaVi. Again.

 

No longer a GuardiaN of the galaxy

Illustration by @NovaH_CS

And with a s1mple snap, NaVi cut 20% of their roster.

A report from French journalist neL suggests that GuardiaN may be leaving NaVi just four months after joining, and taking the AWP off of NaVi's poster child and best player. Funny, that.

NaVi have decided to no longer play 4 on 5 in all their matches (only the ones where flamie can't be arsed) as they hand the AWP back to s1mple and look to bench GuardiaN. To be honest, we didn't even know he was playing in the first place.

The man who once looked like he could kill half the world's population with one shot, now has only achieved killing his own team's chances of winning a tournament, and he didn't even need some fancy glove to do it.

There's no rumours just yet as to who might replace the outward-bound AWPer, as a NaVi Jr. player has been filling in in prac. We wouldn't mind seeing sh1ro, who absolutely obliterated Shara and co. with a 49-9 scoreline yesterday. Not bad.

All jokes aside, we're kinda sad to see GuardiaN fall off this hard. His last chance to win a Major was taken away by Cloud9, which is not the way anyone's career should be remembered, and he's now petered out into being, well, a bit crap, really.

It's making us think our chances of making it pro at our boomer ages might be dwindling.
