Outgamed and out-JAMEd

Source: StarLadder / Igor Bezborodov

We can't remember a Major final that one sided since the last one. Oh, and the one before that. Man, these Astralis finals are so much fun.

device was styling on kids, Magisk brought the fire, dupreeh and Xyp9x went crazy. gla1ve was... well, he was there - and so was his crazy brother. Which is more than we can say about JAME and buster.

If you liked device's pistol round yesterday, you'll love this one, too.

It's hard to be too critical of AVANGAR - the pressure that comes with a Major final, playing some of the greatest ever to touch the game, can't be something they're used to. They're used to playing qualifiers from their own PCs, and they're not even usually that good at those.

Astralis have their fourth Major, and you would imagine, a fourth star on their logo. Only 47 to go before they overtake the USA flag - which is weird, because we're sure Cloud9 only won one Major. Apparently FaZe Clan choked, or something.
