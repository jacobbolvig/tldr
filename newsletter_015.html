Underwhelmingly expected

 

coldzera + FaZe = FaZe era?

Source: @coldzera on instagram

FaZe Clan finally announced coldzera - in maybe the most underwhelming way possible. If you're gonna delay it for that long, you better have an unbelievable announcement ready - in fact, YNk had a great idea. Should have done that, lads.

We, like all of you, were shocked. This was the worst kept secret since Batman used 'Wayne Enterprises' gadgets whilst fighting the bad guys. It got leaked by somebody at ESL, gla1ve and even on YNk's instagram. Are you even trying?

FaZe had a ...cold start, losing 2-0 to Evil Geniuses (this will be explained later). Not all coldzera's fault, though - NiKo went 3-18 on Vertigo.

karrigan fired a shot at NiKo by claiming he had to carry him on FaZe - of course, it was only a warning shot, as in typical karrigan fashion, he just slightly missed the mark. Or he was joking. Probably that one.

Manchester City announced a partnership with FaZe Clan, which is probably where they got the money for this. That makes coldzera the first oil money player in CS:GO.

Not this time either kio
 

The World's unluckiest man

French aficionado neL confirmed that G2 will be signing nexa and huNter- from CR4ZY, which makes this tweet from CR4ZY CEO Antonio Meic look a little silly.

kioShiMa continues his streak of bad luck, however - he is standing in for G2 after missing out on a move to Vitality, and even if he performs well, will not get a spot on a top French team. As if living in a Boris Johnson-led England wasn't bad enough.

G2 could probably use his English skills though - kio, not Boris - as they will now be talking English in game and there are worries JACKZ in particular might struggle. Don't worry G2 fans; they can just use MiBR's example of switching to Englis... oh, right.

The last ninja-defuse
 

Splinter can't save you now, GeT_RiGhT

NiP announced that GeT_RiGhT has been moved to the bench and they believe they have 'found the right person [to replace him]'

According to DeKay, GeT_RiGhT will be replaced by twist - not the Canadian anime character guy, the Swedish one - starting at DH Malmö.

The legendary Swedish bait... sorry, lurker - would like to continue as a player, but he apparently decided to step down to the bench himself. You can't fire me, I quit.

twist was last seen AWPing on Fnatic, where he was replaced by flusha - but he will give NiP a real sniper instead of f0rest picking it up occasionally.

It does mean that Lekr0 will probably continue as in-game leader, which has had, at best, mixed results. Though GeT_RiGhT's stats hadn't been the best of late, it's fair to say the legendary name brought more to the team and he will be missed.

Has anyone not moved?
 

Rostermania!
  • Evil Geniuses have bought the entire NRG roster. We can only assume that EG desperately wanted to get back to never winning T1 tournaments again. Rumours suggest the deal cost around $3m, making FaZe vs EG the first official esports El Ca$hico.
  • Curiously Jacob "Maelk" Toft-Andersen, VP of esports in 100T, said that they had pulled out of the bidding war. He probably still has nightmares about last time he won a bidding war and acquired the North lineup.
  • Furia announced that ableJ has been benched for AWPer HEN1 in one of the weirdest moves of the year. Five year contracts, by the way.
  • G2 announced the departure of shox, who posted a Twitlonger talking about his time, titled 'G2's end'. A bit dramatic mate, you aren't that good.
  • eUnited announced that bodybuilder freakazoid will be taking some time out of his busy gym schedule to replace moose, who mysteriously benched himself.
  • Someone else who'll be moving gym, is Danish strong-man blameF, who's leaving Heroic to join Jason Lake's compLexity. He will be joined by RUSH. Not really the juggernaut you expected, but the team can definitely lift.
  • VPEsports posted an article explaining why Danking got removed from TYLOO after two weeks. He refused to join an official match to finish a game on 5eplay. That's what the kids call a 'bruh moment.'
